Mes Compétences

Front-end

HTML5

Mon premier langage

HTML5 marked the inception of my programming journey. Its simplicity and versatility made it an excellent stepping stone in exploring the world of development. From structuring pages to embedding multimedia, it opened a new realm of possibilities for me.

CSS3

Il n'y a pas d'HTML sans CSS...

Simultaneously with HTML, I dived into CSS, adding aesthetics to the skeletons I built with HTML. It was a fascinating experience seeing how colors, animations, and responsive designs transformed my small projects into visually appealing websites.

JavaScript / ReactJS

Mon coup de coeur

Despite the groans from back-end developers, JavaScript, especially ReactJS, captivated me. This language opened a gateway to dynamic, interactive web experiences, making it my first major infatuation in the programming world.

Back-end

PHP

Mon premiers pas dans le Back-end

PHP was my leap into back-end development. Its logical yet powerful nature allowed me to breathe life into websites, making them responsive and dynamic. With PHP, I truly discovered a great new level of versatility in my skillset, and I knew since there how much I enjoyed back-end.

SQL

Une continuité logique

My adventure into SQL was in parallel with front-end languages, enhancing the robustness of my projects. I found that data handling, manipulation, and structuring were key in creating websites that were functional, adaptable, and responsive.

Docker

Un outil si pratique, mais pas si docile...

When I began working with Docker, it was a revelation. This tool revolutionized my workflow, easing the creation, deployment, and operation of applications. Docker is to deployment what JavaScript was to web interactivity for me.
